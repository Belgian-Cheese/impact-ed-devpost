Why choose Impact Ed

Iris Tracking

Our advanced iris tracking and gesture system revolutionizes accessibility in education. Navigate through lessons, interact with content, and control your learning environment effortlessly using iris gestures only, making education truly inclusive for all users.

AI powered

Experience personalized learning with our AI Teacher, featuring Google's state of the art Gemini model enhanced by RAG. It leverages a 1.6M token dataset for accurate, tailored content adapted to your learning style.

Multilingual

Break language barriers with our extensive multilingual support. Access quality education in 7 Indian languages and 36 foreign languages, making learning truly global and inclusive. Expand your horizons and connect with diverse cultures through education.

Audio Modules

Our Fast Text to Speech conversion technique converts the personalized study modules into audio in an instant, improving the learning experience. This also improves inclusivity by providing an education solution for audio learners.

Voice Enabled SOS

Our Voice Enabled SOS system instantly triggers an alarm when specific emergency keywords are detected. This ensures rapid response in critical situations, providing an added layer of safety for users with limited mobility or communication barriers.

Mental Health Support

Impact Ed recognizes diverse learning needs by offering personalized support for students with mental disorders. Our platform ensures an inclusive, adaptive, and supportive learning environment tailored to every student’s unique challenges.

Our Mission

At Impact Ed, our mission is to revolutionize education by harnessing the transformative power of technology. We believe that learning should be a universal right, not a privilege, and we are committed to creating a platform that empowers individuals of all abilities and backgrounds to reach their full potential. By integrating cutting-edge AI-driven tools, voice interfaces, and innovative iris-tracking technology and audio-conversion techniques, we strive to make education accessible to everyone, including those with physical or mental disabilities. Through a commitment to inclusivity, multilingual content, and personalized learning paths, we aim to break barriers and build a diverse community of learners. At the heart of everything we do is a dedication to affordability and excellence, ensuring that quality education is within reach for all. Together, we can shape a future where knowledge knows no limits and opportunities are endless.
